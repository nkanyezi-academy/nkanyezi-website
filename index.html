Nkanyezi SafePickup

The Problem

Every day, South African schools face the same unanswered question: who is picking up this child — and do they have the right to?

Without a system, any adult can walk in and claim a child. A teacher has seconds to decide. A wrong decision can cost a child their safety. Most schools are still relying on paper registers, memory, and instinct. That is not good enough.

Without SafePickup

A stranger says "I am collecting Amahle today." The teacher checks the paper register. The name is not there. The parent cannot be reached. What happens next?

With SafePickup

The collector enters Amahle's unique PIN. Her photo appears on screen. The teacher verifies the face. A WhatsApp reaches the parent instantly. Safe. Done. Recorded.

01
Photo Verification at Every PickupEvery collection is photographed and time-stamped. No child leaves without a permanent, searchable record.
02
Instant WhatsApp to ParentsThe moment a child is collected, the parent receives confirmation. No uncertainty. No missed calls.

Our Four Divisions

Learn. Create.
Earn. Build.

Four pathways. One mission. Every child and family that comes through Nkanyezi leaves with more than skills — they leave with a mindset that changes their relationship with the digital world permanently.

01 Learn Nkanyezi Learn

Foundational digital literacy, phonics, reading, and coding concepts for children and families. Home of the Little Star Readers Program — a structured phonics intervention for early learners.

02 Create Nkanyezi Create

Animation, storytelling, content creation, and digital art. Where Mama kaNkanyezi — an original South African animated series — and other African stories are brought to life on screen.

03 Earn Nkanyezi Earn

Turning digital skills into sustainable income. Freelancing, online business, and monetisation strategies designed for South African parents and young creators ready to enter the digital economy.

04 Build Nkanyezi Build

App development, digital product creation, and entrepreneurship. Where ideas become real products — like SafePickup, conceived and built within Nkanyezi to solve a real South African problem.
